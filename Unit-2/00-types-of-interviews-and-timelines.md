# Interview Types and Timelines

Most companies have a multi-step interview process, where you go through phone interviews, "culture" interviews, technical whiteboard interviews and / or coding interviews.

* Technical Phone Screen
* In Person Technical Interview
* Take Home Project
* In House Project
* Pairing
* White Boarding
