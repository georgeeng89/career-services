# Preparing for the Job Search


## Prerequisites:

Completion of Unit: Job Application Toolkit

## Unit Objective:

By the end of this unit, you will be able to utilize maximize the effectiveness of your job search by utilizing knowledge of human interaction and by avoiding common pitfalls and mistakes.  

## Unit Overview:

In this unit, you will get a high-level overview of the job search process and tips and tricks for success. You will also learn how growth mindset is important when looking for a job, how to start thinking of the kinds of companies you want to target, and how to manage your time while looking for a job.
